How to make the best Chicken Wings

Recpie

Here's a quicy, easy, and delicious Chicken Wing recipe!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings (bone-in)
  • Frank's red hot
  • Buttermilk
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Aditya's special seasoned flour
  • Butter
  • Ranch (make your own or get Wingstop/Dominos ranch)

Steps

  1. Mix Buttermilk anf Frank's red hot in a bowl. Add wings to mixture and season with salt and pepper. Marinate overnight.
  2. Fill a dutch oven with oil and heat until 350.
  3. While it's heating up, remove the wings from the marinate and coat in Aditya's special seasoned flour.
  4. Place wings in oil for about 10 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. While it's heating, mix 1 cup of Frank's red hot and 1 stick of butter in a medium heat pan
  6. Take wings out, pat dry, and toss them in the sauce
  7. Serve with ranch and enjoy!
